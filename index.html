Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Product Details

What types of steel products do you supply? +
We supply a comprehensive range including MS Channels, MS Angles, Steel Pipes (seamless & welded), Hollow Sections, GI Coils & Sheets, TMT Bars, Steel Plates, Flat Bars, Round Bars, and more. All products comply with international standards including ASTM, BS, EN, JIS, and DIN.
Do you provide custom cutting and fabrication services? +
Yes, we offer custom cutting, bending, and fabrication services according to your specific project requirements. Our advanced machinery ensures precise dimensions and quality finishing.
What standards do your products comply with? +
Our products comply with various international standards including ASTM (American), BS (British), EN (European), JIS (Japanese), DIN (German), API, and IS (Indian) standards. All products come with mill test certificates.

Delivery & Timelines

What are your typical delivery timelines? +
For stock items, delivery is typically within 24-48 hours across major Indian cities. For custom orders or large quantities, delivery takes 3-7 working days. International deliveries are arranged based on location and order size.
Do you deliver outside India? +
Yes, we export to Middle East countries, Africa, and South Asian nations. We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and logistics to ensure smooth delivery to your location.

Pricing & Payment

How can I get a price quotation? +
Simply fill out our quote request form with product specifications and quantity, or contact us directly via phone or email. We provide detailed quotations within 2-4 hours during business hours.
Do you offer bulk discounts? +
Yes, we provide competitive pricing for bulk orders. Contact our sales team with your requirements for a customized quote with the best possible rates.
