Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Product Details

What types of steel products do you supply? +
We supply a comprehensive range including MS Channels, MS Angles, Steel Pipes (seamless & welded), Hollow Sections, GI Coils & Sheets, TMT Bars, Steel Plates, Flat Bars, Round Bars, and more. All products comply with international standards including ASTM, BS, EN, JIS, and DIN.
Do you provide custom cutting and fabrication services? +
Yes, we offer custom cutting, bending, and fabrication services according to your specific project requirements. Our advanced machinery ensures precise dimensions and quality finishing.
What standards do your products comply with? +
Our products comply with various international standards including ASTM (American), BS (British), EN (European), JIS (Japanese), DIN (German), API, and IS (Indian) standards. All products come with mill test certificates.

Delivery & Timelines

What are your typical delivery timelines? +
For stock items, delivery is typically within 24-48 hours across major Indian cities. For custom orders or large quantities, delivery takes 3-7 working days. International deliveries are arranged based on location and order size.
Do you deliver outside India? +
Yes, we export to Middle East countries, Africa, and South Asian nations. We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and logistics to ensure smooth delivery to your location.
How do you ensure safe delivery of steel products? +
All products are professionally packaged and secured for transport. We use specialized vehicles equipped for steel transportation and provide cargo insurance for all deliveries.

Pricing & Payment

How can I get a price quotation? +
Simply fill out our quote request form with product specifications and quantity, or contact us directly via phone or email. We provide detailed quotations within 2-4 hours during business hours.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers, letters of credit (LC), cheques, and cash payments. For regular clients, we offer credit terms based on business relationship and order volume.
Do you offer bulk discounts? +
Yes, we provide competitive pricing for bulk orders. Contact our sales team with your requirements for a customized quote with the best possible rates.

Quality Assurance

Are you ISO certified? +
Yes, we are ISO 9001:2015 certified. Our quality management system ensures consistent product quality and service excellence across all operations.
Do you provide mill test certificates? +
Yes, all our products come with original mill test certificates (MTC) showing chemical composition, mechanical properties, and compliance with specified standards.
How do you ensure product quality? +
We source from certified manufacturers, conduct incoming quality inspections, perform random testing in our laboratory, and maintain strict storage conditions. Every product undergoes quality checks before dispatch.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy? +
Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery if products are found to be defective or not as per specifications. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by original invoice and MTC.
Do you provide warranty on your products? +
We provide manufacturer's warranty against material defects. Warranty terms vary by product type. GI products typically have anti-rust warranty, while structural steel has warranty against manufacturing defects.
What if the delivered product doesn't match specifications? +
If delivered products don't match the agreed specifications, we will immediately arrange replacement at no additional cost. We stand behind the quality of our products 100%.
